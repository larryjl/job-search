## ABOUT SECTION

Most data teams have the same problem: pipelines that nobody fully understands, dashboards that get ignored, and a gap between what the data says and what gets decided. That gap is where I work.

For the past 7 years I've built and fixed data infrastructure across healthcare and technology, writing the SQL, designing the warehouse, building the dashboard, and then sitting down with the people who need to act on it. At QHR Technologies I redesigned ETL pipelines that ran 20x faster, scaled migration capacity from 100 GB to 500 GB per project, and cut turnaround times by up to 50%. At Alberta Health Services and South Calgary PCN, I built data warehouses that consolidated reporting across clinics (20+), and re-engineered legacy reporting processes to output what had previously required two to three analysts.

I'm comfortable on both sides of the table with business stakeholders and engineers to define problems and design solutions. I gather requirements, translate them into specs, build the solution, and explain the output to an executive who doesn't care how the pipeline works, only what it means for Monday's decision.

I'm back on the market after parental leave, looking for mid to senior data analyst, data engineer, BI, or business analyst roles. Remote preferred, Calgary hybrid considered. If your team needs someone who can build the pipeline and present the findings, feel free to reach out.
